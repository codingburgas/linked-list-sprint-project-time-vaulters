Name: The American Revolution
Date: April 19th 1775

The American Revolution was a colonial revolt against British rule, with thirteen American colonies declaring independence and forming the United States of America. Tensions had been escalating due to taxation without representation and other grievances. The conflict began with skirmishes in Lexington and Concord in 1775 and culminated in the Treaty of Paris in 1783, which recognized American independence. The revolution introduced groundbreaking political ideas, emphasizing liberty, equality, and democracy. These principles inspired numerous other movements worldwide and significantly influenced the development of modern democratic societies.
